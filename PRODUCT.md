# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Hiring managers, collaborators, and engineers who land on a personal site to decide whether Segun ships real systems. They scan in seconds on a laptop or phone, looking for proof in products and open-source tools, not a résumé narrative.

## Product Purpose

Personal portfolio for Oluwasegun Olumbe, engineer and founder in London. It presents BashBop, Brain Boost Buddy, Snap A Bird, and the open-source tools (gate, repoctx, tieline, bouncer, aiglare) with equal weight. Success is a visitor leaving convinced he builds both products and tools, and clicking through to the work.

## Brand Personality

Bold, loud, hard to ignore. Direct founder voice: practical stories, no fluff, presence that fills the viewport.

## Anti-references

- The current cream/paper + red accent + mono-label look
- Generic SaaS landings (identical cards, soft gradients, Inter-default typography)
- Terminal/hacker aesthetics (neon on black, green code, monospaced costume)

## Design Principles

1. **Work is the proof.** Products and tools carry the page; decoration never outruns the substance.
2. **Equal weight.** Founder products and open-source tools share the stage; neither is a footnote.
3. **Hard to scroll past.** Scale, contrast, and rhythm should stop a fast scanner.
4. **Personal without soft.** Real stories (2am calls, built for his kid) stay specific; warmth is not the visual system.
5. **Built, not templated.** The site should feel intentional and authored, never like a portfolio theme.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast. Keyboard-reachable links with visible focus. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`. No information conveyed by color alone.
